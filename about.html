About Us

Telling our inspiring story from the very beginning to today

Behind the Success

RAZ Engineering Solutions began with a vision to bring quality, safety, and precision to the industrial engineering space. Founded by Pfanani Razwiedani after years of experience at Eskom and Enserve, our brand was built on hard work and trust.

Over the years, we have expanded our service offerings, our partnerships, and our team — evolving from a focused valve services provider into a full-service engineering company serving diverse industries across Southern Africa.

Pfanani Razwiedani

Founder & Director

Who We Serve

We proudly support industries that depend on operational reliability — including power generation, water processing, manufacturing, and public infrastructure. Whether it’s Eskom or local municipalities, we work hand-in-hand with partners who value quality and compliance.

How We Operate

At RAZ Engineering, we take a hands-on, client-centered approach. Every project begins with an assessment of client needs and ends with certified, documented delivery. We focus on safety, innovation, and teamwork — with qualified technicians and a commitment to excellence.
